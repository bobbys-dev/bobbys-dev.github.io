Serverless dev with Cloudflare Workers
Jan 1, 0001

I recently learned of a neat Cloudflare tool to that lets developers deploy “serverless code”: Cloudflare Workers. It approximates AWS Lambda in being a compute instance that lives in closer to the edge of the users' site, and it’s fast at doing what it does. “Just deploy code” in your choice of language: Javascript, Rust, Python, C/C++… and you don’t have to be mindful or worry about unused capacity, auto-scaling, or load balancing.
